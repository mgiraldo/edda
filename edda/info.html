Edda™ connects you with a friend living far, far away. This app is a bit like a tunnel extending through the earth, which allows the two of you to reach one another. Over time, Edda will help you to embrace the distance that keeps the two of you apart, while making this virtual connection a bit more human. This means aligning your body with someone else's, despite your remote and unique positions, and holding your gaze straight to their eyes, like an offline conversation. More at: www.edda.info.
